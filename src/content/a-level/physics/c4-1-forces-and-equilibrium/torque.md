# Torque

> [Moment](./moment.md) of a couple
>
> $$
> \begin{align*}
>   T &= Fd \\
>   \text{torque} &= \text{force} \cdot \text{perpendicular distance between couples}
> \end{align*}
> $$
>
> Unit of $T$ = $\mathrm{N\,m}$

Always produces rotational effect

## Couple

Pair of **equal** and **parallel** forces
